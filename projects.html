Medisync

Medisync is a workforce management solution designed for healthcare providers to streamline the scheduling and payment of medical staff. The platform allows clinicians to report their availability and select shifts in real time, supporting both hourly-based and fixed-rate services. It features dynamic allocation of shifts across multiple locations, transparent financial transactions, and comprehensive administrative tools for tracking workforce coverage and costs. Key development focused on implementing advanced models for shift availability and integrating fixed-price service reporting to enhance operational efficiency.

  • Market: UK
